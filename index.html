A Seoulful Summer

Last July, my family and I visited Korea for the first time in 10 years. It was an enriching experience being able to reconnect with extended family I hadn't seen since I was a kid, as well as soaking in the culture. Everything from the delicious foods to taking the metro system around the country stirred a feeling of bliss inside me. I invite you into my memory capsule as I walk you through the various parts of my journey this past summer.
